About the City Tour

A Kigali city tour takes you through the bustling streets, modern architecture, vibrant markets, and cultural landmarks that make this city unique in Africa. From the iconic Kigali Convention Centre to the lively Kimironko Market, there is always something new to discover.

Official Tourism Info

Top Attractions

Kigali Convention Centre

An architectural marvel and symbol of Rwanda's development, hosting major international events.

Kimironko Market

Kigali's largest open-air market — perfect for fresh produce, crafts, fabrics, and local street food.

Kigali Skyline Viewpoints

Climb to the hilltop viewpoints for breathtaking panoramic views of the city's rolling hills.

What to Bring on Your Tour

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Sunscreen and a hat
  • A camera or smartphone
  • Rwandan Francs for market shopping
  • A light jacket (mornings can be cool)
  • A reusable water bottle
  • Your passport or ID
